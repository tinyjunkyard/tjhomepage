Sharing makes us all stronger

July 3, 2019 by Greg Willson

Collaborating for a better future.

Tiny?

When thinking of junk, its usually something we want less of, we want it reduced. 'Tiny' refers to the fact that we support a digital junkyard that is in constant state of motion via planning, selling, trading, and maintenance, with recycling or disposal as a last option. Our goal is to divert more and landfill less.

Requesting materials

Tiny Junkyard is a connector that helps us to have virtual space to work from. Alot of the work that needs to be done is basically PLANNING, coming up with a good design... That's why we are working to provide an intelligent junkyard robot assistant. The assistant we are working on is design to help assist each of us here at the Tiny Junkyard with sharing and trading items so that more projects get built .

Our values

Easier ways to share items which could othewise go to waste, good designs, and the benefits of building with your neighbors are the common goals that motivate this project. Trading and collaborating locally creates cohesive community and can make work more enjoyable.
